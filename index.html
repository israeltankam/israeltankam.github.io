Research interests

Mathematical modelling of plant disease dynamics, pest management and demo-genetics for resistance durability; dynamical systems, Bayesian inference, time-series forecasting and decision-support systems for agriculture. Recent applied focuses: potato cyst nematode management, cassava viral epidemics, banana soil-borne pests.
